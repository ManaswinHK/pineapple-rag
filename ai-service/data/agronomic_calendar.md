## Pineapple Crop Cycle (Farm 1 & Farm 3)
*Varieties: MD2 (Highland), Josapine (Coastal)*

1. **Vegetative Growth (Months 0-8):** Focus on nitrogen and foliage expansion.
2. **Flower Induction (Month 9):** Ethephon (hormone) application to force synchronous flowering.
3. **Fruit Development (Months 10-14):** Critical window for Brix (sugar) accumulation and fruit sizing. Stress during this period directly reduces yield and weight.
4. **Harvest Window (Month 15-16):** Harvesting based on color break.

### Current Block Status (Pineapple)
- **Farm 1, Block C-3:** Currently in Month 12 (Fruit Development). Extremely vulnerable to yield loss if stress (like root rot) is not mitigated.
- **Farm 1, Block A-1:** Month 6 (Vegetative). Healthy.
- **Farm 3, Block 12:** Month 14.5. Was scheduled for harvest in Month 15, but salt stress is causing early ripening (survival mechanism). Harvest window shifted 10 days early.

## Oil Palm FFB Harvest Cycle (Farm 2)
- **Harvest Rounds:** Conducted every 10-14 days depending on ripening.
- **Peak Season:** March-May and September-November.
- **Impact of Pests (Bagworm):** Bagworms defoliate the fronds. Oil palms rely on fronds for photosynthesis to produce Fresh Fruit Bunches (FFB). Severe defoliation in Block W-2 will reduce FFB weight for the next 6-12 months if not controlled immediately.
